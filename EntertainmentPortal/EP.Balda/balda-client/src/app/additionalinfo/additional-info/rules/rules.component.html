Game rules

On the picture below you see the field for the game.

  1. Each turn you can add only one letter on the field.
  2. The letter should form a new word (see my example in the next post)
  3. Syllables can be only vertical or horisontal diagonal words are prohibited so the new letter can form a syllable with the letter above, below or on the sides of it.
  4. The goal is to fill the field with words.
  5. You can't repeat the words, if someone wrote room, you can't write room as well even if it will be the word in the another part of the field.
  6. The winner the person who will have more total points by the end of the game.
  7. One point is given for one letter of words you add.
  8. You can't make 2 turns in a row.
